PCOR PDF FILES NEEDED
=====================

Please upload the official PCOR (Preliminary Change of Ownership Report) forms 
for each county to this directory with these exact filenames:

1. los-angeles-pcor.pdf       - Los Angeles County PCOR form
2. ventura-pcor.pdf            - Ventura County PCOR form  
3. san-bernardino-pcor.pdf     - San Bernardino County PCOR form
4. riverside-pcor.pdf          - Riverside County PCOR form
5. orange-pcor.pdf             - Orange County PCOR form

These forms can be obtained from each county's official website or 
assessor's office.

Once uploaded, the system will automatically fill in the property and 
trust transfer information.
